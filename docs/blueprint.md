# **App Name**: MaxilloConnect

## Core Features:

- Lead Capture Form: An interactive form to collect potential clients' full name, phone number, email, and specific checkboxes (cheques) for their inquiries.
- WhatsApp Integration: Dynamically generate a WhatsApp URL pre-filled with the collected form data, redirecting the user to start a conversation with the medical professional upon form submission.
- Personalized Inquiry Generator (AI): An AI tool that suggests a concise, personalized opening message for the WhatsApp chat, incorporating key details from the user's selected 'checks' to streamline the initial consultation.
- Responsive Page Layout: Ensure the landing page content, form, and all elements are optimized and fully responsive across various devices, including mobile phones, tablets, and desktop computers.
- Service Information Display: Dedicated sections to present essential information about the maxillofacial surgeon's services, professional expertise, and clear contact details.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A professional and trustworthy medium blue (#44ACDB). The hue is chosen to evoke a sense of calm and reliability, appropriate for a medical practice.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated blue-gray (#EEF4F6), providing a clean and serene backdrop that complements the primary color and supports readability.
- Accent color: A fresh and bright aquatic green (#69CBB3), designed to draw attention to call-to-action elements while maintaining a sophisticated and modern feel. It provides a distinct contrast in saturation and brightness to the primary color.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, objective, and highly legible appearance, ensuring clarity and professionalism for all textual content.
- Use clean, contemporary line icons that relate to health, communication, and medical precision to reinforce the professional and caring image.
- A minimalist and structured layout that prioritizes content legibility and directs user focus to the lead capture form and the call to action for WhatsApp contact.
- Subtle and elegant micro-animations for form interactions and content transitions, enhancing user engagement without distracting from the core information or professional tone.
